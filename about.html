About us

Daniel N. Muhindi, a seasoned trainer and coach originally from Kenya, brings over two decades of dedicated experience as a marathon runner to his practice. Throughout his extensive career, he has participated in races across Africa, Europe, and the United States, garnering a deep understanding of the sport.

With his wealth of knowledge and expertise, Daniel specializes in guiding individuals of varying skill levels, from newcomers to seasoned veterans, towards achieving their personal best in marathon running. His approach is tailored to meet the unique needs and goals of each client, fostering an environment of support and growth.

Beyond his passion for running, Daniel is committed to promoting holistic well-being. He works closely with his clients to develop comprehensive lifestyle strategies that encompass physical fitness and nutritional health, ensuring they thrive both on and off the track.
