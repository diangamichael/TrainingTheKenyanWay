About us

Kenyan born trainer and coach, Daniel N. Muhindi is an experienced marathon runner of 20+ years participating in races in Africa, Europe and the United States. He helps runners meet their peak potential ranging from first time participants to experienced marathon runners. Daniel also helps his clients achieve a healthy lifestyle including physical fitness and nutrition needs.
